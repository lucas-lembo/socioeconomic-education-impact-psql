Below is a glossary that provides a more detailed explanation of what the tables in our models map:

- Capital Type: The school's area, indicating whether it is located in the Capital or the Interior of a given State.
- Network Type: The administrative dependency of the school, specifying whether it is Federal, Municipal, or State-run.
- Location Type: The school's location, indicating whether it is in a rural or urban area of a given city.
- Student Classification Percentage: The percentage of students in a given school within a certain socioeconomic level (the higher the level, the better the individual's living conditions).
- Approval Rates: The approval rate of students in a high school.
- Grade-Level Approval: The approval percentage for each grade level in high school at a given school.
